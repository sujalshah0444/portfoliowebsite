A SHORT VISIT TO SWAYAMBHUNATH

   "We were breathless and sweating as we stumbled up the last steep steps and practically fell upon the biggest vajra (thunderbolt scepter) that I have
    ever seen. Behind this Vajra was the vast, round, white dome of the stupa, like a full solid skirt, at the top of which were two giant Buddha  eyes 
    wisely looking out over the peaceful valley which was just beginning to come alive.", a book describes the experience of a bunch of tourists
    visiting  Swayambhunath.
     Indeed! It is one of the most beautiful places in the Kathmandu valley to visit.
     A place where you can get a bird's-eye view of the entire valley.


WELCOME TO DHARAHARA

Welcome to  Dharahara! 
    A 72m high tower located in the heart of Kathmandu Valley. Initially constructed for military usage as a watchtower but now used as a view tower.
    One of the oldest architectures of Nepal recognized by UNESCO.
