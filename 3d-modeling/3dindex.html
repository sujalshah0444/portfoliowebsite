PHOTOGRAMMETRY & 3D MODELING


Welcome to the process of creating three-dimensional representations of an object or a surface. 3D models are made within computer-based 3D modeling software, which we'll explore a little later. During the 3D modeling process, you can determine an object's size, shape, and texture.


3D modeling helps us view the world from a different perspective. It is like watching everything from a bird's-eye view as you can view it from all angles.


For more extensive uses, Civil engineers may use 3D models for surveying and other construction works. It is mainly used during the surveying of rural locations, dense forest areas, and other locations with a high degree of risk or uncertainty. Whereas Computer engineers may modify "Meshroom" & "Blender", open-source softwares, to further improve the debugging the bugs related with those softwares, and add customized features, which will help for the improvement of those softwares.

For all of my projects, softwares like Agisoft Metashape Pro, Meshroom, Adobe Premiere Pro, Blender, Adobe Photoshop, etc. were used.
